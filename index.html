Achievements

The Best School For Exemplary contribution in the field of Education Award

Jhps got this award in the year 2019

Jubilee Hills Public School has been a pioneer in taking initiatives to make learning impactful and fun. With a staunch belief in ‘Technology and Tradition’, JHPS has moved ahead with the best of technology and books keeping the focus student-centric in its intrinsic terms. A slow but steady progress supported with hard work and patience has surfaced to parallel with dynamic techniques relevant to the current scenario. To honour and recognize this continual strife towards excellence, Jubilee Hills Public School has been conferred with ‘The Best School For Exemplary contribution in the field of Education’ at the Times Excellence Award 2019-20,Hyderabad.The award was presented to the Chairman,Jubilee Hills Public School, Shri A Murali Mukund and The Secretary, Jubilee Hills Education Society, Shri T.Harischandra Prasad by Sri Jayesh Ranjan, Principal Secretary of the Industries & Commerce (IT) Departments of the Telangana State. It is an honour and excellent achievement for JHPS, indeed, as it is one of the most coveted amongst the recent string of awards bestowed on JHPS.






Brain feed school excellence Award

Jhps got this award in the year 2017

It was a proud and momentous occasion for Jubilee Hills Public School to be awarded in three categories in the top 500 schools of India of the Brain Feed School Excellence Awards for the year 2017-2018. The triple awards were: Best Academic Excellence; Infrastructure and Special Education. Jubilee Hills Kindergarten School too received the Brain feed- School Excellence Award among the top 100 Preschools of India (2017- 2018). The Academic Director Ms. G. Sree Devi received the award on behalf of the school at the Brain Feed 5th national conference on 12th November, 2017 at Bangalore. "






World Education Summit Award

Jhps got this award in the year 2017

Jubilee Hills Public School garnered international recognition with a jury is choice award at the World Education Summit, Dubai held on 26th- 27th November, 2017. JHPS was honoured for Introducing Impelling Innovations in Teaching Pedagogy. The jury’s opinion was that the teachers of Jubilee Hills Public School focus on every student and the assessment and evaluation approach of the school helps the children develop holistically. The World Education Summit is co-hosted by the Knowledge and Human Development Authority, Govt. of Dubai. JHPS is the only school from South India to have been conferred the Award amongst a few other schools from the rest of India, Netherlands, Singapore, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Qatar and Dubai. The award was received by The Academic Director, Ms. G. Sree Devi. The Academic Director was also one of the speakers on the panel speaking on “Green Schools: Future of Global schools”.

  • School has recently opened a new canteen

    My school has recently opened a new canteen. Nowadays,It is perhaps the most popular place in the school.   • Our school has implemented online attendance managment system

    Online Attendance Management System is software developed very recently in our school for daily student attendance in schools, colleges and institutes. It facilitates to access the attendance information of a particular student in a particular class. The information is sorted by the operators, which will be provided by the teacher for a particular class. This system will also help in evaluating attendance eligibility criteria of a student. Since ages, attendance system has remained one of the most important systems for evaluating the working time of students in any college or school. In short, this project is used to mark the number of days present/absent in any academic year of students in a college, school etc.
